Maanasa Sivashankar (Mahn-sa)

Specialist in Service Design & Strategy

Finding the institutional root causes of customer issues — and designing the operations that fix them.

I am a designer, researcher, and urbanist leading the service design team at the Mayor's Office of Innovation. I set team direction in partnership with the Director, established the hiring criteria for the design practice, and helped build the broader MOI team — the product designers and data experts I work alongside on each engagement. The work focuses on the Mayor's top priorities, guiding city services to be more effective and efficient. Before joining MOI, I worked in New York City as a consultant on Fortune 100 organizations such as Johnson & Johnson, J.P. Morgan Private Bank, Penske Trucking Company, Meta, Google, and as an in-house consultant for The World Bank.
